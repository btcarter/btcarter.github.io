Human Physiology Lab

Course Summary

Purpose

The purpose of this course is simple: to safely demonstrate the governing principles that enable human life through the use of effective model systems, simulations, experimentation, and discussion; to give students the knowledge, skills and confidence to predict the function of and manipulate physiological systems to meet specific goals in conjunction with a team of colleagues, thereby preparing them for a more in depth study of physiology as they prepare to be researchers and health care providers.

Learning Objectives

At the end of this course students should have achieved the following outcomes:

  1. Know the basic laws by which human physiology is governed.
  2. Demonstrate the ability to manipulate a physiological system to attain a predetermined goal.
  3. Be adept at working in a team to both learn and explore principles that are difficult to understand and support each other in the investigative process.
  4. Have confidence in their understanding of human physiology and the skills needed to manipulate it.
